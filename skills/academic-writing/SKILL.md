# Academic Writing Skill

## Purpose

Produce clear academic prose suitable for finance journals.

Writing style should resemble papers in:

* Journal of Finance
* Review of Financial Studies
* Journal of Financial Economics

---

# Writing Guidelines

Use precise terminology.

Avoid marketing language.

Focus on mechanisms and results.

Structure arguments logically.

---

# Section Templates

## Introduction

1. Research question
2. Economic importance
3. Mechanism
4. Contributions
5. Literature positioning

---

## Model Section

Describe:

agents
information
equilibrium

Define notation clearly.

---

## Results Section

Present propositions.

Explain economic intuition.

Highlight mechanisms.

---

## Literature Review

Group papers by mechanism rather than chronology.

Explain differences between models.

---

# Output Style

Write in academic tone.

Use short paragraphs.

Avoid excessive jargon.

Prefer clarity over verbosity.
