# Referee Simulator Skill

## Purpose

Simulate referee reports similar to those received at finance journals.

Focus on:

novelty
identification
model assumptions
literature overlap

---

# Review Structure

## Summary

Brief description of the paper.

---

## Main Contributions

Evaluate novelty and importance.

---

## Major Concerns

Examples:

* unclear equilibrium definition
* unrealistic assumptions
* insufficient differentiation from literature

---

## Minor Comments

Formatting suggestions.

Clarity improvements.

---

## Recommendation

Possible recommendations:

Reject
Revise and resubmit
Accept with minor revisions
