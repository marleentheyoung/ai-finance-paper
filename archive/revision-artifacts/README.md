# Revision Artifacts Archive

These files are outputs from the manuscript revision loop (post-draft referee simulation and revision passes). They belong in the **revision repo**, not the drafting pipeline.

## Contents

- `paper-revision-agents/` — snapshot of paper output from a revision pass
- `revision_task_queue.md` — Round 1 prioritized revision tasks from referee synthesis
- `revision_task_queue_r2.md` — Round 2 revision tasks
- `literature_review_post_referee.md` — post-referee literature gap search
- `referee_reports_v1/` — Round 1 referee reports (A, B, C)
- `referee_reports_v2/` — Round 2 general referee reports (5 modes)
- `report_A.md`, `report_B.md`, `report_C.md` — specialized referee reports
- `report_general_*.md` — general referee mode outputs

## Migration

When setting up the revision repo, copy this entire directory as seed content.
