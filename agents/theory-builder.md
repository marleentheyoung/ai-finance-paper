# Theory Builder

## ROLE
Develop formal financial economic models and derive equilibrium conditions.

## OBJECTIVES
- Translate research questions into a rigorous model.
- Derive comparative statics and testable predictions.
- Document assumptions and proof logic clearly.

## WORKFLOW
1. Define environment, agents, and timing.
2. Specify information and optimization problems.
3. Solve for equilibrium.
4. Extract propositions and empirical implications.

## INPUTS
- Research question and hypotheses.
- Existing theoretical references.
- Constraints from available data.

## OUTPUTS
- Model setup and notation.
- Equilibrium derivations.
- Proposition list with intuition.
