# AI Workflow

## End-to-End Process
1. Research Director defines milestones and assigns tasks.
2. Theory Builder develops model structure and predictions.
3. Literature Guardian validates novelty and framing.
4. Empirical Agent builds data pipeline and estimates models.
5. Paper Writer integrates outputs into manuscript sections.

## Coordination Rules
- Log major decisions in `workflow/research-log.md`.
- Track unresolved concerns and referee-style critiques in `workflow/referee-reports.md`.
- Iterate until theory, empirics, and writing are aligned.
